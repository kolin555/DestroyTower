This folder contains AO bakes and UV maps for the models included in this pack. You can use them to make your own textures if so desired.

Some attachments share textures with others, or with the weapon to which they are attached. In Volume 2 - Heavy Weapons, these models share textures:

* Minigun - [None]
* Rocket Launcher - Base weapon + Laser
* Quad Rocket Launcher - Base weapon + Scope + Shoulder rest
