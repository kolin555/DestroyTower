This folder contains AO bakes and UV maps for the models included in this pack. You can use them to make your own textures if so desired.

Some attachments share textures with others, or with the weapon to which they are attached. In Volume 1 - Small Arms, these models share textures:

* Pistol - Base weapon + Clip; Sight + Flashlight
* Revolver - Flashlight + Laser
* Uzi - Clip + Scope + Crosshair; Folding stock + Hinged stock
